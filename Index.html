About KKRS Sangha

Who we are and what we stand for

KKRS Sangha is a registered association for Christian Reddy families in Bengaluru and around the world. Established on 11 June 2011, we were founded with a simple belief: when our community stands together in faith and service, every family grows stronger.

Read Full Story →

🕊

Our Vision

To build a united, service-oriented Christian Reddy community that preserves our cultural identity, deepens our faith, and ensures no member is left behind.

🤝

Our Mission

To bring Christian Reddy families together through fellowship, cultural programmes, and mutual support — while helping members access opportunities for education, health, and financial growth.

✝

Our Faith

Rooted in Christian values of love, service, and community, we celebrate both our faith and our Reddy heritage, passing them on to the next generation.
